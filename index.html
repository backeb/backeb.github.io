The through-line

These aren't stages I moved through — they're four things I do at once. The leverage is in the connections between them, and that intersection is the vantage point I bring as a strategic advisor.

Academic research

Numerical ocean modelling and satellite remote sensing, from ocean–climate dynamics to operational forecasting.

Applied science

Turning models into tools — search-and-rescue trajectories, automated workflows, global flood mapping.

Project leadership

Coordinating large international consortia across EO, cloud infrastructure and digital twins.

Product management & development

Taking science-based services from prototype to lasting, societal-scale impact.

Where it converges

I bring these together as a strategic advisor — at the intersection of science, technology, and the decisions they inform.
